About HomeLabDeals

What We Do

HomeLabDeals is a curated marketplace for storage hardware — hard drives, SSDs, and related tech — specifically selected for homelab enthusiasts, data hoarders, and IT professionals.

We cut through the noise of endless product listings to surface the deals that actually matter: high-capacity drives at the best price-per-GB, enterprise gear at consumer prices, and storage solutions that stand up to 24/7 operation.

Our Audience

  • Homelab Builders — Running Proxmox, TrueNAS, Unraid, or self-hosted services
  • Data Hoarders — Archiving media, datasets, and personal cloud storage
  • IT Professionals — Building test environments and backup infrastructure
  • Small Business Owners — Cost-effective storage for on-premise servers

Demographics: Predominantly US and EU-based, technical decision-makers, high purchase intent, research-heavy buying process.

Traffic & Reach

HomeLabDeals launched in early 2025 and is actively growing through:

  • Organic search (homelab storage, best NAS drives, cheap hard drives)
  • Reddit communities (r/homelab, r/DataHoarder, r/HomeServer)
  • Direct referral from related tech sites

Why We Exist

Buying storage shouldn't require a spreadsheet. We do the math — price-per-GB, reliability ratings, use-case fit — so you don't have to.

Every drive we list has been vetted for:

  • Price competitiveness
  • Reliability (Backblaze stats, user reports)
  • Availability from reputable sellers
  • Appropriate use case (NAS vs. cold storage vs. boot drive)
